Welcome to Crystal Connections

Crystal Connections is a cooperative game of meaning-making, inspired by the spirit of the Glass Bead Game and designed as an open artifact for cultural creators. In this game, you and your fellow players collaboratively build a web of concepts, linking them through knowledge, metaphor, real-world references, and imagination. The goal is not to "win," but to grow a rich and inspiring tapestry of ideas together.

Use these tables as a resource to guide your connections during play. You can either print these pages directly from your browser or download them as a PDF for easier access. Feel free to add your own entries and contribute back to the project!

How to Use the Tables

Each game of Crystal Connections begins by selecting a Starter Seed — a concept that will grow into your web of ideas. Roll two six-sided dice to choose a row and column from the Starter Seed Concept Table and place your starting idea into the central node of the board.

Then, as the game unfolds, use the other tables to inspire and extend your connections:

  • Field of Knowledge — Connect through an academic or cultural domain.
  • Perspective Shift — Reframe the concept in a new symbolic or metaphorical way.
  • Real-World Anchor — Tie the concept to real events, places, or traditions.
  • Future Seed — Imagine a possibility growing from the idea into the future.

Each table is a 6x6 grid. Roll two six-sided dice to select a row and a column, then interpret the result in a way that meaningfully connects to the current concept on the board.

✨ Empty spaces are intentionally left for future contributions. Cultural creators are encouraged to suggest new entries and expand the living body of Crystal Connections!

Crystal Connections is an Open Artifacts Project. Contribute new ideas, tables, and expansions at: https://CrystalWordChain.com
